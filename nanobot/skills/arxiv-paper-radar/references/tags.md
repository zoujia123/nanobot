# Tags

Assign up to 3 strong tags per relevant paper. Do not add weak tags.

- `serving`: online LLM serving or inference systems
- `training`: LLM training or fine-tuning systems
- `offline`: offline or batch inference
- `thinking`: reasoning-model inference or test-time compute systems
- `RL`: reinforcement learning, RLHF, or post-training infrastructure
- `MoE`: mixture-of-experts training or serving
- `RAG`: retrieval-augmented generation infrastructure
- `video`: video generation model systems
- `multi-modal`: multimodal model systems
- `sparse`: sparsity methods with systems impact
- `quantization`: quantization methods with systems impact
- `offloading`: KV cache, activation, parameter, or weight offloading
- `hardware`: accelerators, hardware architecture, or hardware-aware systems
- `storage`: storage, checkpointing, data pipeline, or I/O optimization
- `kernel`: CUDA, GPU kernels, operators, compiler lowering, or low-level runtime optimization
- `diffusion`: diffusion model systems
- `agentic`: systems for agentic workloads or long-running agents
- `edge`: edge, mobile, or heterogeneous device inference
- `networking`: network transfer, communication, all-reduce, RDMA, or routing
- `disaggregation`: prefill/decode, attention/FFN, or other disaggregated serving designs
- `scaling`: autoscaling, capacity planning, routing, or cluster resource scaling
